Chicken Marbella

Chicken Marbella is a classic dish originating from the iconic Silver Palate Cookbook. This Mediterranean-inspired recipe features succulent chicken marinated in a tangy-sweet mixture of prunes, olives, capers, and garlic, then baked to perfection with white wine and brown sugar.
